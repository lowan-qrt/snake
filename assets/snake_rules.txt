Snake game rules (version with bombs)

1.  The player controls a snake that moves continuously.
2.  The snake grows with each piece of food it eats.
3.  If the snake touches a wall or its own body, the game is
  over.
4.  Bombs appear randomly on the field.
5.  A bomb disappears after a short delay if it has not exploded.
6.  If the snake touches a bomb, the game is lost instantly.
7.  The goal is to survive as long as possible and get the
    highest score.
